Request Account Deletion

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your ALAS Browser account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

What happens when I delete my account?

ALAS Browser is built with privacy in mind. We do not have any remote servers holding your browsing data. Your "account" profile and your "Secure Sync" data backups are securely encrypted and stored locally on your device's internal storage.

  • Your Profile Data: Your email address, display name, and authentication token will be erased from the app's internal database.
  • Encrypted Backups: Any browsing data you chose to back up using the "Secure Sync" feature will be permanently erased from your device's isolated application storage.
  • Local Browsing Data: Deleting your account will optionally also let you clear your local tabs and bookmarks from the database.

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your local encrypted backups are deleted, they cannot be recovered by anyone.

How to delete your account inside the app

The fastest way to delete your data is directly within the ALAS Browser application:

  1. Open ALAS Browser on your device.
  2. Tap the Menu icon and go to Settings.
  3. Navigate to the Account section.
  4. Select Delete Account & Data to automatically erase your profile and sign out of the app.

Data Retention Policy

Because your user data is stored strictly on your device, we retain zero personal browsing data. If you submit a manual deletion request via email for analytics purposes, we will process and purge your email identifier from any analytics registries within 14 days.
