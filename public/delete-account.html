Data Privacy & Safety

Request Account Deletion

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your ALAS Browser account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

What happens when I delete my account?

ALAS Browser keeps most browsing data on-device by default. Your account profile is stored in the app's local storage. If you use Secure Sync, encrypted backup data may remain on-device or may be stored in a separately configured sync backend for your signed-in account.

  • Your Profile Data: Your email address, display name, avatar reference, and local account session are removed from the app.
  • Synced Backup Data: The app attempts to delete the encrypted backup tied to your account from the configured backup location available to this build.
  • Local Data Cleared: Account deletion clears local bookmarks, open tabs, browsing history, quick links, and sync preferences on this device.
  • Not Automatically Cleared: Downloaded files stored outside the app, website cookies and local site storage, and saved passwords or autofill entries may remain until you remove them separately.

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once an encrypted backup is deleted from the available backup location, it cannot be recovered by us.

How to delete your account inside the app

The fastest way to delete your data is directly within the ALAS Browser application:

  1. Open ALAS Browser on your device.
  2. Tap the Menu icon and go to Settings.
  3. Navigate to the Account section.
  4. Select Delete Account & Data to remove the account, clear local account-linked browsing data, delete the configured backup for that account when available, and sign out of the app.

Alternative Methods

If you no longer have the app installed, Android usually removes app-private local data from that installation. However, uninstalling alone does not guarantee deletion of any remote account or encrypted backup records that may exist for a configured sign-in or sync backend.

Data Retention Policy

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it, clear the account in the app, or uninstall the app. If a configured remote sync backend was used, we aim to remove the backup tied to your account when the in-app delete flow succeeds or when we process a valid manual request. Manual email requests for remaining account-related or analytics-related records are handled within 14 days. Downloaded files saved to shared storage are not removed by an email request.
