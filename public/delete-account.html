Data Privacy & Safety

Request Account Deletion

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your ALAS Browser account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

What happens when I delete my account?

  • Your Profile Data: Your email address, display name, and authentication link (Google Sign-In) will be permanently deleted from our servers.
  • Cloud Synced Data: Any browsing data you chose to back up using the "Secure Sync" feature (such as bookmarks or history) will be permanently erased from our secure cloud database.
  • Local Data: Deleting your account does not automatically clear the browsing data stored locally on your physical device. You can clear your local data directly within the ALAS Browser app settings.

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your synced data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

How to delete your account inside the app

The fastest way to delete your data is directly within the ALAS Browser application:

  1. Open ALAS Browser on your device.
  2. Tap the Menu icon and go to Settings.
  3. Navigate to the Account section.
  4. Select Delete Account & Data to automatically erase your synced profile from our servers and sign out of the app.

How to request account deletion via Email

If you no longer have the app installed, or prefer that we handle the deletion for you, you can submit a data deletion request via email.

Please send an email to our support team from the email address associated with your ALAS Browser account:

  • To: subhajitu51@gmail.com

  • Body: Please include a brief message stating that you want to delete your ALAS Browser account and erase all associated cloud sync data.
Send Deletion Request Email

Data Retention Policy

Once we receive your verified deletion request via email, we will process it and permanently delete your account and associated synced data from our active databases within 14 to 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.
